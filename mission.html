Our Mission

As we power up our devices every morning and faithfully check our favorite news sites at intervals throughout the day, we are bombarded by a constant drumbeat of bad news. It's hard to believe, though, that the human condition could be so hopeless. There must be plenty of people out there impacting the world for the good, but we don't hear enough about them. This site will restore your faith in humanity, in progress and in the inherent potential for goodness and creativity we all possess within.
